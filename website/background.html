Background

Rising sea levels is the issue where oceans are rising is caused by one root issue, Global Warming. Global warming is caused by people releasing greenhouse gases which causes heating. This heating causes the oceans to warm expanding it and also causes ice sheets to melt which adds water into the ocean.
